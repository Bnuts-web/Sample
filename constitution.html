BNUTS Constitution

“We, the Birifor students... united by our shared identity, language, and aspirations.”

Adopted: February 10, 2023 | Motto: “Empowering Identity, Promoting Excellence”

Our Core Identity

BNUTS is a non-partisan, non-religious, and non-profit union of Birifor students in tertiary institutions across Ghana and beyond. Our official colors are Navy Blue and Gold.

Key Objectives

  • Unite all Birifor tertiary students nationwide
  • Advocate for academic, social, and welfare needs
  • Promote and preserve Birifor language and culture
  • Foster leadership, mentorship, and peer support
  • Encourage civic engagement and community service

Membership

Open to all Birifor students enrolled in accredited tertiary institutions. Members have the right to participate, vote, and stand for office — and the duty to uphold BNUTS values.

National Executive Committee

The leadership team includes:

  • President
  • Vice President
  • General Secretary
  • Financial Secretary
  • Women’s Commissioner
  • Organizing Secretary

Officers serve a 1-year termtwo consecutive terms.

Branches

Campus branches can be formed with 5+ Birifor students. Each branch operates under this Constitution and elects its own leadership.

Meetings & Governance

  • General Assembly: Held at least once per year — all members may attend and vote.
  • Executive Meetings: Monthly coordination by national officers.
  • Decisions: Made by majority vote (constitutional amendments require 2/3 majority).

Finances

Funded through voluntary dues, donations, and events. All finances are managed transparently, with quarterly reports to the Executive and annual reports to members.

Amendments

This Constitution may be amended by a two-thirds majority at a General Assembly, with proposals shared at least 14 days in advance.
