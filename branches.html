As a growing and dynamic student organization, the Birifor National Union of Tertiary Students (BNUTS) is proud to have established strong and active branches across various tertiary institutions in Ghana. Each branch plays a critical role in representing Birifor students at the local level, fostering unity, and promoting academic and social development. Our nationwide presence ensures that no Birifor student is left behind, no matter where they study.

University of Ghana, Legon
The flagship BNUTS branch, known for its vibrant student activities, cultural events, and strong advocacy for Birifor students' rights and welfare.
AAMUSTED - Kumasi Campus
Active BNUTS chapter promoting Birifor unity and academic excellence at the Akenten Appiah-Menka University of Skills Training and Entrepreneurial Development.
