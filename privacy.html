Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

The Birifor National Union of Tertiary Students (BNUTS) is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website, membership forms, events, or communications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Student index number
  • Tertiary institution and program of study
  • Contact details (email, phone number)
  • Hometown or Birifor community
  • Photos (for membership cards or event documentation, with consent)
  • Feedback or messages submitted via our contact form

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely for BNUTS-related purposes, including:

  • Processing and managing your membership
  • Communicating about events, meetings, and opportunities
  • Advocating for Birifor student welfare in academic institutions
  • Promoting cultural identity and academic excellence
  • Improving our services and outreach
