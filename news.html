April 10, 2025

BNUTS Launches Academic Mentorship Program

In a bid to support first-year Birifor students, BNUTS has launched a nationwide academic mentorship program pairing senior students with juniors across 8 institutions. The initiative aims to improve retention, build confidence, and foster peer learning.

“Education is our collective responsibility,” said Amos Kuudeerma Kador, BNUTS President. “This program ensures no Birifor student walks alone.”

March 22, 2025

Birifor Language Workshop Draws 120+ Students

The inaugural BNUTS Birifor Language & Culture Workshop held at UDS-Nyankpala brought together over 120 students from 5 campuses. Participants learned basic phrases, traditional songs, and the importance of linguistic preservation in academic spaces.

Plans are underway to host the event quarterly at rotating campuses.

February 15, 2025

BNUTS Signs Partnership with Ghana Tertiary Education Commission

BNUTS has signed a landmark Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Ghana Tertiary Education Commission (GTEC) to advocate for inclusive language policies and support for minority ethnic student groups in higher education.

This partnership marks a major step in institutional recognition of Birifor student needs.

January 30, 2025

Member Spotlight: Victoria Boitey Wins National Leadership Award

BNUTS Women’s Commissioner, Victoria Norma Boitey (KNUST), has been awarded the 2025 National Student Leadership Excellence Prize for her work in promoting gender equity and cultural identity on campus.

“This is a win for every Birifor girl with a dream,” Victoria said.
