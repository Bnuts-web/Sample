About BNUTS

The History of BNUTS

The Birifor National Union of Tertiary Students (BNUTS) was officially established in February 2023 as a unifying and representative body for all Birifor students enrolled in tertiary institutions across Ghana and beyond. Born out of a vision to amplify the voice and presence of Birifor students in higher education, the Union was spearheaded by Mr. Benjamin Sande, whose foresight recognized the critical need for collective representation and purposeful engagement.

BNUTS was formed through the collaborative efforts of pioneering students from diverse academic institutions including universities, teacher training colleges, nursing schools, and technical universities, who shared a common goal: to uplift the Birifor identity while fostering academic excellence and unity among members.

Since its inception, the Union has positioned itself as a student-centered movement, dedicated to:

  • Advocating for the educational and welfare needs of Birifor students
  • Championing inclusive participation in national and institutional discourse
  • Cultivating leadership, cultural pride, and social empowerment among members

With a commitment to “Empowering Identity, Promoting Excellence,” BNUTS continues to be a driving force for progress, bridging gaps, building networks, and shaping a brighter future for Birifor students everywhere.

Our Vision Statement

BNUTS envisions a future where every Birifor student in tertiary education thrives in an environment of equal opportunity, academic excellence, and inclusive support. We aspire to build a strong, united community that empowers students to become impactful leaders, cultural ambassadors, and agents of sustainable development within Ghana and beyond.

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to serve as the leading voice for Birifor tertiary students by:

  • Advocating for their rights, interests, and overall well-being across all institutions.
  • Fostering an inclusive community that celebrates cultural identity and values the diverse experiences of every student.
  • Providing innovative and practical support to address academic, financial, and social challenges faced by members.
  • Promoting leadership, entrepreneurship, and civic engagement as tools for personal and community transformation.
  • Building strategic partnerships with stakeholders in education, government, and industry to advance equity, sustainability, and opportunity in higher education.
